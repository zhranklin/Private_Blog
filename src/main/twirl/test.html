报告人简介：

Huazhu Fu is a Research Scientist in Institute for Infocomm Research, at Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR), Singapore. He received the Ph.D. degree from Tianjin University in 2013. He worked at Nanyang Technological University (NTU) for two years as a postdoctoral research fellow. His research focuses on computer vision, image processing, and medical image analysis. He has published more than 20 papers in IEEE Transactions journals (TIP, TNNLS, TMM) and top tier conferences (CVPR, ICCV). His PhD dissertation was nominated for China Computer Federation (CCF) Outstanding Dissertation in 2014.
